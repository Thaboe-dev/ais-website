## What happened

On January 15, 2026, AIS opened the Spring 2026 semester with a fireside chat featuring **Dr. Jesse Thornburg**, Assistant Teaching Professor at CMU-Africa and AIS Faculty Advisor — under the theme **"Lessons from the Frontlines of Innovation."**

### What was covered

Dr. Thornburg drew from his work at the intersection of **technology, education, and entrepreneurship** to discuss:

- How innovators **navigate uncertainty** in the early stages of a venture
- How to **manage failure** without letting it derail momentum
- The **role of academic institutions** in supporting research commercialization
- Why **adaptability** matters more than the original plan

### Who it was for

The session was particularly valuable for first-year students just beginning to think about how their academic training connects to real-world impact — and what their next two years at CMU-Africa could look like.
