## What happened

On June 27, 2025, AIS members participated in the **EdTech edition of Founders' Friday** at **Norrsken House Kigali** — co-organised by **Norrsken Rwanda** and the **ICT Chamber**.

### What was covered

- **Early-stage EdTech ventures** building in the Rwandan and East African markets
- **Investor perspectives** on what makes EdTech investible at the seed stage
- **Policy considerations** for technology-enabled education

### What AIS took back

The session broadened members' understanding of Rwanda's EdTech landscape, surfaced the structural challenges of building sustainable education ventures, and seeded interest in linking future AIS programming to digital education themes.
