## What happened

On October 22, 2025, AIS partnered with the **AWS Cloud Club** to host a Tuesday Innov session on **Empowering African Businesses with Generative AI on AWS** — led by **Stacy Cheruto**.

### What was covered

- A **technical primer** on generative AI services available on AWS
- **Application-focused** patterns for African business use cases
- Live exploration of the developer tooling that makes GenAI accessible to early-stage teams

The session was the kind of cross-club collaboration AIS leans into — pairing a generalist entrepreneurship community with deep-technical communities on campus.
