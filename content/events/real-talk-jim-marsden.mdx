## What happened

On February 17, 2026, AIS launched its new **Real Talk** series with **"Beyond the Hustle"** — an unfiltered fireside chat with **Jim Marsden**, Executive Coach, Master Wilderness Guide, and Theory U Practitioner. The session was moderated by **Prof. João Barros**, Associate Director and Research Professor at Carnegie Mellon University.

### Why "Real Talk"

The series was created as a deliberate counterweight to the conventional entrepreneurship narrative. Less polished pitch theatre. More honest conversation about what it takes to build well — and stay whole while doing it.

### What was discussed

- A **critique of "hustle culture"** as the default narrative for young African founders
- More **sustainable, purposeful, human-centered** approaches to leadership
- Insights from Marsden's unusual blend of **executive coaching, wilderness leadership, and presencing theory**
- Prof. Barros's grounding of those ideas in the **realities of building tech ventures in African contexts**

### Why it resonated

Together, the two speakers created the kind of reflective dialogue that's rare in business education — and exactly the kind of programming AIS wants to keep delivering through Real Talk.
