## What happened

On February 7, 2026, AIS hosted an **exclusive live webinar** featuring **Iyinoluwa Aboyeji** — CEO of **Future Africa** and co-founder of **Andela** and **Flutterwave** — themed **"Building Africa's Innovation Ecosystem: Strategies, Stories, and Scalable Solutions from Africa's Leading Innovators."**

### Impact

Aboyeji is one of the most influential voices in African technology. Through Andela he reshaped the global pipeline of African engineering talent; through Flutterwave he helped build the rails for African digital payments; and through Future Africa he now backs the next wave of African founders.

### What was covered

- The current **state of Africa's startup ecosystem** — what's working, what isn't
- The role of **patient capital** in supporting early-stage African ventures
- Why young African technologists must position themselves as **builders, not consumers**, of technology
- The **concrete moves** an early-stage African founder can make to attract serious investors

### Impact

The webinar was particularly impactful in motivating CMU-Africa students to think beyond employment outcomes — and toward venture creation as a viable path out of school.
