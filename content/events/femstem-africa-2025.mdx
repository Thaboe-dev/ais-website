## What happened

On June 23–24, 2025, AIS supported CMU-Africa students at the **FemSTEM Africa 2025 Pitch Competition** — the culmination of a women-in-healthcare innovation series hosted by the **African Leadership University** and the **Health Innovation Hub (H2i)**, supported by the **Mastercard Foundation** and **Sustineri Attorneys PRUC**.

### AIS-supported projects

- **MamaMind** — a postpartum mental-health platform for new mothers
- **SafeSpeak** — an anonymous digital mental-wellness tool for women

### The win

**SafeSpeak placed 3rd overall**, earning a **$500 cash prize** and recognition for AIS members as emerging healthtech innovators.

### What it unlocked

The competition seeded a strategic relationship with the **Africa Health Collaborative** — opening pathways for further venture support and research collaboration around the SafeSpeak and MamaMind projects.
