## What happened

From **November 17–21, 2025**, AIS supported the **Industry Innovation Lab (IIL)** in delivering **Global Entrepreneurship Week (GEW) 2025** at CMU-Africa.

### AIS's role

- **Student mobilisation** across CMU-Africa cohorts
- **Inter-university outreach** to ALU, RICA, the University of Rwanda, and other Kigali partners
- **On-the-day logistics** for sessions, panels, and pitches across the week

### Programme

The full week covered:

- Founder-led sessions with operators across multiple sectors
- Panel discussions on the state of African entrepreneurship
- Pitch events and showcases for student ventures

GEW 2025 reaffirmed CMU-Africa's role as a convening hub for entrepreneurship education in Kigali — and AIS's role as the student spine that makes large weeks like this one work.
