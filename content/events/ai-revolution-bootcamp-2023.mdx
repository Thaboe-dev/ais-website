## What happened

On December 6, 2023, AIS ran the **AI Revolution Bootcamp for Students** — a deep-dive on how to use AI responsibly as an entrepreneur, researcher, and student. The session was attended by **30 students**.

### The lead

The bootcamp was led by **Prof. João Barros** — founder and former CEO of **Veniam** and **Streambolico**, professor of ICT, AI, Business and Policy, and now Associate Director at CMU-Africa.

### What students took away

- A working understanding of generative AI tools and their real-world applications
- A framework for the **ethical application** of AI in entrepreneurship and research
- Practical examples of how AI changes day-to-day building for early-stage founders
