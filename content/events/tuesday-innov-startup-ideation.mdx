## What happened

On September 30, 2025, AIS opened its Fall 2025 Tuesday Innov series with a **Startup Ideation and Validation Workshop** — hosted by **Ulrich Armel Codjia**, AIS Minister of Partnerships.

### Format

A hands-on, fast-paced session focused on:

- **Generating** startup ideas worth working on
- **Refining** them into testable hypotheses
- **Validating** them quickly with real users — before building anything

### Why these sessions exist

Tuesday Innov is the bi-weekly heartbeat of AIS — informal gatherings where students working on ventures share progress, get peer feedback, and stay accountable to their commitments between bigger AIS events.
