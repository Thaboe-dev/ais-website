## What happened

On November 11, 2025, AIS hosted a fireside chat with **Albert Munyabugingo**, CEO of **VubaVuba** — one of Rwanda's most prominent on-demand delivery startups. The session was facilitated by **Prof. Jesse Thornburg** and run as part of the **Foundations of Entrepreneurship** course at CMU-Africa.

### What was covered

- The **practical mechanics** of building a venture from scratch in an African market
- **Scaling lessons** — when to invest in operations vs. growth
- The **founder–operator** transition and how Albert manages it at VubaVuba

### Cross-pollination

Linking AIS programming to a foundational entrepreneurship course expanded the reach of the conversation beyond AIS's regulars — bringing students who don't normally attend club events into direct contact with a working African founder.
