## What happened

On March 23, 2024, AIS hosted its **first-ever training bootcamp** focused on rapid prototyping. The event brought together **60 students** from:

- **Carnegie Mellon University Africa**
- **University of Rwanda**
- **African Leadership University**
- **Kepler College**

### The format

The day combined two complementary tracks:

- **Design thinking** session led by **Prof. João Barros** (CMU-Africa)
- **Hands-on FlutterFlow training** by experts from the FlutterFlow visual development platform — enabling students to ship across iOS, Android, Web, and Desktop

### The output

Working in **multidisciplinary teams of ten**, students developed **six prototypes** addressing real societal and business challenges:

- A real estate agency platform
- A skill-gap solution targeting unemployment
- A seamless grade transfer tool
- An e-insurance platform
- A target-saving platform
- A personal expense tracker

Solutions were pitched to a panel of judges who provided constructive feedback, with selected teams continuing into a fall design-thinking and business strategy workshop.
