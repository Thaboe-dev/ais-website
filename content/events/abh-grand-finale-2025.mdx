## What happened

On March 14–15, 2025, AIS coordinated CMU-Africa's official student delegation to the **Africa's Business Heroes (ABH) Grand Finale** at the **Kigali Convention Centre** — one of the continent's most prestigious entrepreneurship competitions, founded by Jack Ma's foundation.

### The delegation

- **25 CMU-Africa students** attended
- **100% attendance** on both days
- Full logistics — transport, registration, attendance management — handled by AIS

### What students experienced

- Pitch sessions from the **top 10 finalist African founders**
- Panel discussions with **leading African investors and operators**
- Direct **networking** with continental ecosystem leaders

### Impact

The delegation strengthened CMU-Africa's visibility within the African startup ecosystem and gave students front-row exposure to high-growth, impact-driven African ventures — at the scale and stage they themselves are building toward.
