## What happened

On **Saturday, 28 February 2026**, AIS hosted its **flagship event of the academic year** — the **AIS Leadership Summit** at **Carnegie Mellon University Africa**, under the theme **"Forging Resilient Futures: Tech-Enabled, Human-Led."**

The Summit was a half-day of high-impact strategy and networking with leaders shaping the future of the continent, exploring the powerful intersection of digital innovation and visionary leadership.

### Meet our distinguished speakers

Our lineup featured a diverse group of experts from industry and academia:

- **Phionah Ingabire** — Entrepreneurship Consultant, Allan & Gill Gray
- **Millicent Malinga** — Lima Aja
- **Victor Olufemi** — Echo
- **Elie Habimana** — Norrsken East Africa
- **Stephen David** — AIS, CMU-Africa
- **Léandre Berwa** — SLS Energy
- **Dr. Jesse Thornburg** — CMU-Africa
- **Prof. Keith Straughan** — CMU-Africa

### What was covered

As Africa navigates a rapidly evolving landscape, the Summit explored the powerful intersection of **technology and human leadership** — how the continent's next generation of innovators can build ventures that are simultaneously technically advanced and deeply human-centered. Sessions covered:

- **Resilience** in entrepreneurship across multiple economic cycles
- The role of technology in solving **distinctly African challenges**
- The **leadership qualities** required to scale ventures across the continent
- Career pathways at the intersection of academia, industry, and startups

### Event details

- **Date:** Saturday, 28th February, 2026
- **Time:** 12:30 PM – 5:00 PM
- **Location:** Carnegie Mellon University Africa, Kigali

### Impact

The Leadership Summit was one of the **largest single events AIS has organised to date** — a signal of the club's growing capacity to convene senior industry leaders alongside faculty and students in a structured, high-impact format.
