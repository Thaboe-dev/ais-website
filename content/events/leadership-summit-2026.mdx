## What happened

On February 28, 2026, AIS hosted its **flagship event of the academic year** — the **AIS Leadership Summit**, a half-day conference under the theme **"Forging Resilient Futures: Tech-Enabled, Human-Led."** The Summit drew **100+ participants** and featured **9 speakers** from across the African innovation ecosystem and CMU-Africa's academic leadership.

### The line-up

- **Phionah Ingabire** — Allan & Gill Gray
- **Millicent Malinga** — Lima Aja
- **Victor Olufemi** — Echo
- **Elie Habimana** — Norrsken East Africa
- **Stephen David Mhya** — AIS, CMU-Africa
- **Léandre Berwa** — SLS Energy
- **Dr. Jesse Thornburg** — CMU-Africa
- **Prof. Keith Straughan** — CMU-Africa
- **Prof. João Barros** — CMU-Africa

### What was covered

The Summit explored the intersection of **technology and human leadership** — how Africa's next generation of innovators can build ventures that are simultaneously technically advanced and deeply human-centered. Sessions covered:

- **Resilience** in entrepreneurship across multiple economic cycles
- The role of technology in solving **distinctly African challenges**
- The **leadership qualities** required to scale ventures across the continent

### Why this one mattered

The Leadership Summit was the **largest single event AIS has organised to date** — a signal of the club's growing capacity to convene senior industry leaders alongside faculty and students in a structured, high-impact format.
