## What happened

On November 20, 2024, AIS celebrated **Global Entrepreneurship Week (GEW)** with the **Jenga Hackathon final pitch** — a one-day flagship event themed **"Entrepreneurship is for Everyone."** The event drew **103 students from 7 universities** across Kigali and aligned with **Rwanda's Vision 2050**, the **Africa Agenda 2063**, and **SDGs 4 (Quality Education) & 8 (Decent Work)**.

### Programme

- **Opening Remark** by Muhammad Aliyu, AIS Founding President
- **Keynote** by Maria Mayanja, AIS Administrative Advisor
- **Panel discussion** featuring industry leaders from **Afya Wave**, **kLab**, and **BPN Rwanda**
- **Jenga Hackathon Pitch** finale, judged on relevance, feasibility, and impact

### Winning teams

1. **TechTitans (CMU-Africa)** — AI for education access *(1st place)*
2. **Agri-IOT Pioneers (RICA)** — IoT-based agricultural solutions *(2nd place)*
3. **PlantGuard AI (ALU)** — Scalable AI for environmental sustainability *(3rd place)*

### Impact

Prizes ranged from JBL speakers and headphones to branded laptop bags. More importantly, the **winning teams advanced to a 3-month mentorship programme** through CMU-Africa's Industry Innovation Lab — turning a one-day pitch into a proper runway.
