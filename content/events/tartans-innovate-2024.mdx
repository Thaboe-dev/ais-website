## What happened

From **September 6–12, 2024**, AIS supported the **Tartans Innovate Hackathon** — a six-day campus hackathon with **120 CMU-Africa students** forming multidisciplinary teams to build tech-enabled solutions to real campus challenges. The event was co-organised with CMU-Africa's **Industry Innovation Lab (IIL)**.

### Top 5 projects

Out of **23 submissions**, the top five solutions were:

1. **OLGA** — AI-based academic planning assistant *(1st place)*
2. **Casacol** — Housing platform for non-residential students *(2nd place)*
3. **Tartan Ride** — Real-time bus shuttle service *(3rd place)*
4. **Rafiki** — Mental health management tool
5. **SafeSpeak** — Anonymous reporting platform

### Prizes

Winners received **international conference tickets, tech gadgets, and vouchers**.

### Impact

Beyond the prizes, the hackathon gave 120 students hands-on experience in **market validation, prototyping, and pitching** — and reinforced CMU-Africa's culture of multidisciplinary, solution-oriented collaboration.
