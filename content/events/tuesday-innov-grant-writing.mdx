## What happened

On November 25, 2025, AIS closed its Fall 2025 Tuesday Innov series with a fireside chat on **Grant Writing Tips for Early-Stage Founders** — led by **David Masoperh**, Co-Founder and CEO of **Pebla** and an alumnus of the AIS community.

### What was covered

- How to **identify** the right grants for your stage and sector
- The **structure** of a competitive grant application
- **Common mistakes** that disqualify applications before they're read
- How to position African ventures convincingly to global funders

### Why it landed

David came up through AIS himself — making this less a guest lecture and more an alumni paying it forward. That's exactly the alumni-to-founder loop AIS is designed to compound.
