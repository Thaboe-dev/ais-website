## What happened

On December 4, 2023, AIS hosted a fireside chat with **Dr. Steve LaVelle**, founder of Oculus Technologies, attended by **50 CMU-Africa students**.

### The conversation

Dr. LaVelle's talk exposed students to:

- The full arc of building **virtual reality** and **robotics** companies
- Where the opportunities lie for African founders entering deep-tech industries
- How to translate research-grade ideas into market-ready products

The session closed with a live virtual reality demonstration — a hands-on experience that brought the conversation to life for the room.
